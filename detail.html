Mercedes Benz R3

The Mercedes-Benz R-Class is a mid-size luxury multi-purpose vehicle (MPV) that was produced from 2005 to 2017. Combining elements of a minivan, SUV, and luxury sedan, it was designed to offer versatility and comfort for families and individuals seeking a spacious vehicle with premium features. The R-Class was available in both standard and long wheelbase versions, providing ample interior space with seating configurations for five, six, or seven passengers. Its design featured a sleek exterior with a prominent grille and wrap-around tail lights, while the interior boasted luxury appointments such as heated seats and advanced infotainment systems. The R-Class offered a range of engine options, including V6 and V8 variants. Notably, the R 63 AMG 4MATIC featured a hand-built 6.2-liter V8 engine producing 503 horsepower, capable of accelerating from 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) in 4.6 seconds.
